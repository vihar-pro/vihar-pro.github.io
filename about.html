ESTD 1979




CBIT was established in 1979. The institute was initially affiliated to Osmania University, with three undergraduate programmes and an intake of 200 students. By 2014 this had grown to nine undergraduate and seven postgraduate courses with a total intake of 9000 students. It was inaugurated by late Dr. Marri Chenna Reddy. The founder and the first principal of the college was Vundela Malakonda Reddy. CBIT is the first private engineering college in Telangana region
